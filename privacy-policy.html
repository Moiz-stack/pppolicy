Privacy Policy for Pixel Drift Run

Last updated: May 22, 2026

At Pixel Drift Run, accessible from within the Application, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our players. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is NOT collected and recorded by Pixel Drift Run and how we protect your privacy.

100% Privacy & Offline Guarantee:
Pixel Drift Run is a fully offline application. It does not require an internet connection to function, and it does not connect to any servers or external databases. Your game progress, high scores, and coins are stored strictly locally on your device.

1. Data Collection

Pixel Drift Run does NOT collect any personally identifiable information from its players. We do not ask for, or store, your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal data. Your game progress, high scores, and in-game coins are stored strictly locally on your device's internal database for your own viewing and gameplay.

2. Data Usage

Since we do not collect any data off-device, no data is used, processed, or shared with third parties. Your entire gaming experience within the app stays completely enclosed on your hardware.

3. Information Permissions

The Application does not request permissions that would compromise your privacy, such as access to your location, contacts, camera, or microphone. It does not even request the "Internet" permission since it explicitly does not need it to properly function.

4. Third-Party Services

The Application does not include any analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking libraries that could monitor your interaction or activity. Your gameplay is completely private.

5. Children's Privacy

Pixel Drift Run does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app is inherently safe and strongly recommended for all ages to enjoy fast-paced arcade gaming.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. However, any changes will always respect the core principle of maintaining a zero-data, completely offline experience.

7. Contact Us
